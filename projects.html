Dr. Mend

I created this medical diagnosis website with my group as our final project for Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program 2018. "Dr. Mend" is an interactive website that allows users to diagnose themselves with potential diseases and get helpful advice. It implemented ApiMedic's Symptom Checker API, which allows users to enter their age, gender, and symptoms to get a diagnosis. The Advice section gives users advice on many topics, from love to family.
